ProLink Network [PIN]

LEVERAGING OUR COMBINED STRENGTHS AND RESOURCES TO DRIVE MUTUAL GROWTH AND PROSPERITY.

Overview

ProLink Network is a business collaboration platform designed to help entrepreneurs grow through structured networking, referrals, and mutual accountability. The core goal of the network is to connect business owners from diverse industries and foster growth by actively promoting each other’s services.

Core Principles
  • Mutual Promotion: Every member is responsible for ensuring they benefit from others—and that others benefit from them—by actively promoting and referring businesses within the group.
  • Business Diversity: When you join, you are grouped with business owners from different sectors. This creates broader networks and more referral opportunities.
  • Referral-Driven Growth: Members are encouraged to stay alert to business opportunities and be the first to recommend fellow members whenever a relevant opportunity arises.
Accountability and Reporting

To ensure growth and transparency, each member is required to submit a monthly report that includes:

  • Number of businesses received
  • Number of businesses referred or given
  • Total income earned from ProLink referrals

These reports help track individual performance and the overall impact of the network on members’ businesses.

Due Diligence and Reputation

Because members act as promoters of one another’s businesses, due diligence is critical. Before referring any business, members must ensure:

  • The business can deliver as promised
  • The referral will not compromise their personal or professional reputation

Members must understand that their name is at stake with every recommendation they make.

Business Eligibility Checks

Before a business is accepted into the network, ProLink conducts a thorough evaluation that includes:

  • Standard of business premises
  • Quality of products and services
  • Background check for criminal records
  • Any other factor that could affect the credibility or trustworthiness of the business

If a business does not meet the required standards, the owner is guided on areas for improvement. They may reapply after 21 days once improvements are made.

Due diligence should be made by members who are conversant with that business field.

Membership Requirements
  • A monthly membership fee of Ksh 1,000 is mandatory.
  • Attendance at monthly meetings is compulsory.
    • These meetings are essential for sharing updates, building stronger relationships, and fostering continuous growth.
Conclusion

ProLink Network is more than a business referral group—it's a community built on trust, accountability, and collective success. By promoting each other, conducting due diligence, and sharing measurable results, members contribute to a powerful ecosystem where businesses thrive through mutual support and integrity.
