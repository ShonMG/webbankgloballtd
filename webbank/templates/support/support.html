Frequently Asked Questions
Loans

How do I apply for a loan?

You can apply for a loan through your Loans page by clicking 'Apply for Loan'. Ensure you meet all eligibility criteria including minimum share contribution and good credit standing.

What documents do I need for a loan application?

You'll need a valid ID, proof of income, bank statements, and collateral documents if applicable. For larger loans, additional guarantors may be required.

How long does loan approval take?

Loan applications are typically processed within 5–7 business days after all required documents are submitted and verified by our credit committee.

Shares

How do I purchase shares?

Navigate to the Shares section in your dashboard and select 'Purchase Shares'. Minimum purchase is KES 1,000 (10 units). Payments can be made via M-Pesa, bank transfer, or cash deposit.

When are dividends paid?

Dividends are paid annually, typically in the first quarter of each year after the annual general meeting and financial audit.

Can I use shares as loan collateral?

Yes, you can use your shares as collateral for loans up to 80% of their current value. This provides better interest rates and higher loan limits.

Account

How do I update my profile information?

Go to your Profile page and click 'Edit Profile' to update your personal information. Some changes may require verification by our admin team.

How do I reset my password?

Use the 'Forgot Password' link on the login page or visit Account Settings to change your password. You'll receive a reset link via email.

How do I become a guarantor?

Contact our office to learn about guarantor requirements. You must be an active member in good standing with sufficient shares to qualify as a guarantor.

Security

Is my financial data secure?

Yes, we use bank-level encryption and security measures to protect all member data and transactions. Our systems are regularly audited for security compliance.

How do I enable two-factor authentication?

Go to Account Settings > Security & Privacy and toggle on Two-Factor Authentication. You'll receive verification codes via SMS for additional security.

What should I do if I suspect unauthorized access?

Immediately contact our support team and change your password. We'll help secure your account and investigate any suspicious activity.
