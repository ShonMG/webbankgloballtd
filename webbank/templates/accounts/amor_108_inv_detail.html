Introduction

With 1 plus 1 principle (1+1), AMOR 108 is determined to bring financial independence to its members. We bring people together to build their finances, invest together and thrive together. Formed by an idea, we can grow together, AMOR 108 bring together members in pools with the same financial muscles. From as low as Ksh 50 contribution per day to Ksh 10000 per month, AMOR 108 guarantee its members to grow their finances by easy and open management of their contributions, reinvesting the interest earned and growing of each members shares.

Loans are instant and members enjoys 1 percent per month on long term loan and 10 percent per month on emergency loans. The interest is distributed among members and is free to be withdrawn at the close of the year or can be reinvested for higher share rate. Non-members gets loans through members at a rate of 20 percent per month. Repeat clients are adviced to register as members to enjoy more lower interest as they continue to save.
